Moonkeep Support

Last updated: April 7, 2026

If you need help with Moonkeep for iPhone, want to report a bug, or have a privacy-related question, contact the developer by email.

Contact

Support email: tuzzz2685@gmail.com

What to include in your message

  • Your app version and iPhone model.
  • Your country or region.
  • A short description of the issue or request.
  • If relevant, the steps that led to the problem.

Privacy requests

If your request is about privacy or data rights, please mention that in the subject line. Because Moonkeep stores most usage data on your device by default, many deletion requests can be handled directly in the app by clearing local data. If you turned on the optional Experience Improvement Program during first launch or later in Settings, you can also turn it off in Settings to stop future summary uploads.

Current app behavior

  • Moonkeep does not require an account.
  • Most app data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • You can optionally share de-identified session summaries to help improve the app.
  • You can clear local app data from the in-app privacy screen.
