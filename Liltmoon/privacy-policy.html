Liltmoon Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Liltmoon is an iPhone sleep support app designed to reduce bedtime rumination and help users handle nighttime awakenings with lower-friction interventions. In the current version of the app, data created during use stays on your device by default. If you choose to sign in with Apple, Liltmoon stores the Apple account identifier and any name or email that Apple shares during authorization on your device so the app can remember that sign-in on the same iPhone. If you choose to enable the optional Experience Improvement Program, the app uploads a de-identified session summary after each completed session. Raw free-form text and raw event logs are not uploaded as part of that program.

1. Who we are

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to contact the developer of Liltmoon, you can reach us at tuzzz2685@gmail.com.

2. Data processed on your device

Liltmoon may store and process the following categories of data locally on your device:

  • Settings and preferences, such as session duration and related app configuration.
  • In-app records, such as morning feedback, review history, and local event logs.
  • On-device derived signals used to personalize the experience on that same device.
  • If you choose Sign in with Apple, the Apple user identifier and any name or email Apple returns during authorization.

This local processing helps the app preserve your settings, show your history, and improve the effectiveness of interventions on your device.

Liltmoon does not currently use Sign in with Apple to create a cloud account or sync your sleep history to our servers. In the current version, Apple sign-in is used only to authenticate your profile on that device.

3. Experience Improvement Program (optional)

Liltmoon does not upload session summaries by default. On first launch, you can choose whether to turn on the optional Experience Improvement Program, and you can change that choice later in Settings. If you enable the program, the app uploads a de-identified session summary after each completed session. That summary may include:

  • A random app-scoped install identifier so we can understand repeat use on the same installation without using your Apple account, phone number, or contact information.
  • Session configuration and high-level usage metrics, such as planned duration, feature interaction counts, completion state, and exit method.
  • Intervention and decision metadata, such as intervention type, routing confidence, decision basis, and score summaries used to improve intervention quality.
  • Categorical next-morning feedback, if you choose to provide it.

This summary is designed not to include:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any contact information.
  • Advertising identifiers, vendor identifiers, or system-provided device identifiers.
  • Precise location information.
  • Any free-form text you enter.
  • Raw event logs, raw state snapshots, or raw topic labels selected in the app.

The Experience Improvement Program is off by default until you actively turn it on. You can enable or disable it at any time in Settings. Disabling it immediately stops future uploads and clears the pending upload queue stored on your device.

4. Data we receive directly

We may receive personal data only if you choose to contact us directly, for example by email. In that case, we may receive your email address and the contents of your message so we can respond.

5. How we use data

  • To provide app functionality on your device.
  • To remember your preferences and support on-device personalization.
  • To respond to support or privacy inquiries you send to us.

If you enable the Experience Improvement Program, uploaded de-identified summaries are used only to:

  • Analyze intervention effectiveness across different scenarios and improve the product experience.
  • Improve the accuracy of night-waking routing and intervention strategies.
  • Identify and fix product issues.

We do not use this data for advertising, user profiling, or sale to third parties.

6. Legal bases for users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland

Where applicable, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the app features you choose to use.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain the app, respond to inquiries, and protect the service.
  • Consent: where required by law for any optional future collection or diagnostics features.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have privacy rights under applicable law.

  • If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, these may include rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and withdrawal of consent where consent applies.
  • If you are in certain U.S. states, including California, these may include rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of sale or sharing where applicable.

Because most Liltmoon data stays only on your device, in many cases the fastest way to exercise control is to use the app’s local deletion tools. If you contact us about a privacy request, we will respond based on the data we actually have access to.

8. Sale, sharing, advertising, and tracking

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your app data for third-party advertising, and we do not use cross-app tracking for targeted ads.

9. Data retention

  • On-device app data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the app.
  • De-identified data uploaded through the Experience Improvement Program is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to analyze product performance, improve interventions, and operate the service. Because this data uses a random app-scoped identifier instead of your real-world identity, we may be able to analyze repeat usage from the same installation without knowing who you are.
  • Support emails and related correspondence may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and keep support records.

10. Children

Liltmoon is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent. If you believe a child has provided us personal data directly, contact us and we will review the request.

11. Third-party infrastructure

The optional Experience Improvement Program endpoint is hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure. As with most internet services, infrastructure providers may process network-level request data as part of delivering the service.

12. Changes to this policy

If Liltmoon introduces additional data collection features beyond those described here, we will update this Privacy Policy before those changes take effect and seek any consent required by applicable law.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact tuzzz2685@gmail.com.
